Son Irremplazables ("They are Irreplacable")

I built the website to serve as a platform to shed light on the organization Son Irremplazables ("They are Irreplaceable" in Spanish). Son Irremplazables is a grassroots initiative committed to raising awareness about the issue of missing individuals in the Dominican Republic. By highlighting the disappearances and their impact, the website aims to bring attention to this pressing social concern, advocate for solutions, and support affected family members while they search for their loved ones.

  • See it live!

Son Irremplazables Missing Persons Database BETA

I have developed a pioneering website to establish a comprehensive missing persons database for the Dominican Republic. Collaborating with Son Irremplazables, we are working towards making an alpha version of this database a reality in the near future. The website, built using a technology stack that includes React, AWS S3 buckets, AWS PostgreSQL, Heroku, Django, and Redux, is poised to become a tool in aiding missing person searches and reunifications.

Rootin' 4 Us

I developed the product manager and storefront applications for Rootin' 4 Us, a grassroots organization in Los Angeles dedicated to reducing food insecurity. The product manager, built with Flask, ensures secure user registration, login, and product management. It serves as an API for the React storefront application, which offers a user-friendly shopping experience, including dynamic cart functionality and flexible item quantity adjustment. These online applications enable socially distanced orders, improve accessibility, and support local farmers, contributing to the local economy and community well-being.
